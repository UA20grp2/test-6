Shark Man

Yang Zhao is a product designer and graduated from the center of Saint Martins in 2016 and creates an experimental design called Sharkman. The Sharkman is a portable piece of furniture that explores the possibilities of deformable soft furniture. It brings the ability to create your own flexible private space to play in, work,... around the user. The person can lie, sit or stand. It can be modified to create total privacy or full exposure to others. The design was selected to be exhibited at the Brainwaves London Design Festival. It has won the Certificate of Excellence from the Mayor of London's International Student Innovation 2016 Award.
