Scout Jobertus

As a space for the installation, we have chosen the main Scouts Jobertus at Brasschaat. The room is located in the park of Brasschaat and has a wooded Environment. The materialization of the room consists of different materials and can be parted in 2 different compartments. On one side you have the bar with the seating area and on the other side is the dance space/ storage space. What also strikes you directly is the frequent use of bricks, which are used for all walls. Furthermore, you have the bar that is made by means of fast-build concrete bricks. The ceiling is made of dark wooden slats and the floor is made of red ceramic tiles. Furthermore, there are a few glass walls in the walls. In the second part, the dance room of the mains room lie again ceramic tiles, this time with a light-gray color. In terms of colours, we find mostly dark shades such as red brown and black. As far as light is concerned, the main focus is on artificial light from fluorescent lamps and spotlights. Natural daylight can also penetrate the space, but only if the wooden shutters are opened at the large windows along the outside. The acoustics in this room are pretty bad. There is a large reverberation and if there are many people in the room it is not pleasant to sit.
