Meaning of the logo

For our logo we have chosen an abstract form of the scout's greeting, because the symbolism of this gesture is related to the corona crisis. This fits in with our project, because this is exactly what we have done. We made the influence of Covid-19 visible through a scouting room.
The greeting means that we mean well together. The 3 fingers refer to a vow. With this vow a scout says to obey the law, to help others and if he chooses so, to honor the duty/service to God and country. During the corona crisis it is also very important to adhere to corona measures and to be there for each other. For us, the last finger refers to the care that is devoted to our country every day.
In addition, you also have the little finger under your thumb. This position symbolizes that the strong must protect the weak. We all follow the corona measures to protect each other and especially the people at risk.
The greeting is completed with a firm handshake from the left hand, at which point the thumb and little finger cross.
