FOMU

As a space for our installation we have chosen a large hall from the photo museum in Antwerp.
Our project is about the uncomfortable feeling of the scout room where a bustle and presence that is needed suddenly falls away completely. For this we also wanted to mirror the room in disproportion in relation to the other space. The room in which this is placed must therefore certainly be large enough. Originally it was divided into two by a partition wall, which we omitted for the final realization as the two smaller spaces were not sufficient. However, this was only a minor adjustment. The room has no windows and is thus only illuminated with light fixture. This has to do with the fact that the space is designed for posting photos and natural light might damage it. All artificial light is directed at the works in a beautiful way by means of spotlights. They then reflect on the white walls that are present in each room.
